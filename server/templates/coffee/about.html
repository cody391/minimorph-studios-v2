Where It Comes From
We know who grew every bean we serve.

Sourcing transparently means paying farmers a price that reflects the actual cost and quality of their work — not commodity market price. We visit farms when we can, and we work with importers who share that same commitment.

What's on our bar changes with the harvest cycle. When a great lot is gone, we find the next great lot — we don't substitute with something lesser to keep the menu consistent.

How We Roast

Light enough to taste where it came from.

We roast to highlight the origin character of each coffee — not to cover it up. Our espresso roast is developed just far enough to bring out sweetness and body while keeping the brightness of the origin intact. Our filter coffees are roasted lighter, designed to reward slower brewing methods like pour-over and cold brew. Everything is roasted in small batches and rested the right amount of time before it goes on bar.
