### Key Messaging Pillars

1. **Earnings transparency** — Always show real numbers. People respond to specific dollar amounts.
2. **No cold calling** — This is the #1 objection to sales jobs. Lead with it.
3. **AI tools** — Differentiator. Most sales jobs don't give you AI assistance.
4. **Flexibility** — Remote, set your own hours. Appeals to side-hustlers and full-timers.
5. **Gamification** — The leveling system is unique. Highlight the progression.
6. **Professional brand** — MiniMorph is premium. Attract people who want to represent quality.
