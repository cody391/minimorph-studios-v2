# 01 — Global Context

## What MiniMorph Studios Sells

MiniMorph Studios sells **premium, done-for-you websites** with white-glove delivery, ongoing support, and business growth systems — all under a recurring monthly contract.

The customer pays once to get started and then pays a monthly retainer. MiniMorph builds, hosts, maintains, and improves the site on their behalf.

## Customer-Facing Positioning

- **DO say:** "Premium done-for-you website. We build, host, maintain, and grow it for you."
- **DO say:** "White-glove service. You talk to us, we handle everything."
- **DO say:** "Built for your specific business. Not a template. Not a builder."
- **DO NOT say:** "AI-built website."
- **DO NOT say:** "Automated website generation."
- **DO NOT say:** "We use ChatGPT/Claude to write your site."
- **DO NOT imply:** the customer is getting a low-cost AI product.

The AI-assisted generation is an **internal fulfillment tool only**. It produces a first draft. A human admin reviews, edits, and approves every site before the customer sees it. The customer never learns the internal stack.

## Business Model

| Component | Detail |
|---|---|
| Product | Done-for-you website + ongoing maintenance + support |
| Pricing | Monthly contract (starter, growth, premium tiers) |
| Sales channel | Sales reps (commission-based) — later system |
| Fulfillment | Internal team + AI-assisted generator |
| Support | Support ticket system + owner direct response |
| Growth | Lead nurturing, upsells, anniversary check-ins |
| Hosting | Cloudflare Pages per customer site |

## Current Priority (as of 2026-05-15)

**Finish the website generator first.**

No other system matters until:
1. The generator produces honest, clean, complete sites
2. The contact forms on every generated site actually work
3. A controlled first customer can be taken through the full cycle
